FAQ

How can I reach Capital Fort?

+

Capital Fort is located on Tsarigradsko Shose. You can take the Metro (Station Tsarigradsko Shose) or use the underground parking if you're coming by car.

Is the event in English?

+

The event is accessible to both English and Bulgarian speakers: In-person: Sessions are held mostly in Bulgarian. Online: The stream features multi-language subtitles to accommodate our international audience.

Where is the venue and is there parking?

+

The venue is easily accessible via the "Tsarigradsko Shose" metro station, and paid underground parking is available for attendees arriving by car.

Will there be a social part after the sessions?

+

Yes! The event concludes with an evening cocktail session at Barter Community Hub. It’s a great opportunity to enjoy drinks and high-level networking with a stunning night view of the city.

What is the event schedule?

+

Tech Bridge 2026 is an evening event. Registration begins at 16:45, followed by keynote sessions and panels. We will move to the Barter Community Hub for networking from 21:00 onwards.

Can I watch the sessions if I cannot attend in person?

+

Yes, we offer a Virtual Pass that gives you high-definition live stream access to all keynote speeches and panels, as well as the ability to participate in the digital Q&A sessions.

Will I receive a certificate of attendance?

+

Absolutely. Every attendee will receive a digital certificate of attendance, highlighting the key topics covered (AI, Web3, W and Cybersecurity), which can be shared on LinkedIn.
